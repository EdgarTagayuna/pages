Monster Encounter: Slime Monster

As you walked deeper into the pathway, you could hear slimy noises inside the walls. From afar, you saw blue, viscous substance seeping out
from the crevices of the wall and of the floor. Gradually, it formed into a huge, translucent blob. It was blocking the path. However, it didn't seem to be
harmful until it shot out a slimy substance, grazing the edges of your sleeves. You noticed that it had acidic properties, causing them to be damaged.
It kept firing, but you evaded them with ease. What will you do now, traveler?
